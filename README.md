‘Ick’ or Imagination? Women’s AI Fears and Fantasies in the AI Boom

Inspired by gender gaps in AI adoption and discourse around AI “ick,” this project investigates how women frame artificial intelligence through narratives of fear and possibility. Using YouTube transcripts and comments from women-hosted podcasts about AI from 2022-2025, the study maps how attention and emotional framing are expressed by and around these communities during the AI boom. 

Core research question: What specific AI use cases do women imagine for themselves, and how does that imaginative framing shift across the 2022–2025 AI boom?
